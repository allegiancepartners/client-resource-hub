What Happens at Each Stage

  1. Founder — initiates & receives

    The process starts and ends with you. You kick things off by sharing your company and goals, and at the end of the process the finished Toolkit is delivered back to you — the founder is both the client and the source of truth throughout.

  2. Intake — Founder Discovery Form

    We capture your incorporation date, ownership structure, cap table, and target exit timeline through a structured discovery form. This is the raw material every later calculation is built on, so accuracy here matters more than speed.

  3. Planning Engine — Financial Model · Road Map · Appendices

    Your intake data feeds our planning engine, which builds out a full financial model, a step-by-step road map for pre-exit and post-exit planning, and supporting appendices — QSBS timing, trust structures, and charitable strategy included.

  4. Toolkit — client-facing deliverable

    Everything is compiled into your Founder Exit Toolkit — a clear, personalized package showing what your specific exit nets after tax across multiple scenarios, ready to guide the decisions ahead.
